## **2\. The Data We Collect About You**

### **What Information Do We Collect?**

Personal data means any information about an individual from which that person can be identified. We may collect, use, store and transfer the following types of personal data:

* **Identity Data:** First name, last name, nickname, username, title, gender  
* **Contact Data:** Billing address, delivery address, email address, telephone number  
* **Profile Data:** Purchases, orders, interests, preferences, feedback, survey responses  
* **Transaction Data:** Payment details, products and services purchased  
* **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our website, app, products and services  
* **Technical Data:** IP address, login data, browser type and version, time zone, location, browser plug-ins, operating system, platform  
* **Marketing and Communications Data:** Marketing preferences, communication preferences, correspondence with us

### **Aggregated Data**

We also collect, use and share aggregated data such as statistical or demographic data. This is not considered personal data as it does not directly or indirectly reveal your identity.

### **What We Don't Collect**

We do not knowingly collect:

* Special Categories of Personal Data (race, ethnicity, religious beliefs, sexual orientation, political opinions, health data, etc.)  
* Information about criminal convictions and offences

## **9\. Your Legal Rights**

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

1. **Request Access** \- Receive a copy of the personal data we hold about you  
2. **Request Correction** \- Have incomplete or inaccurate data corrected  
3. **Request Erasure** \- Ask us to delete or remove personal data where there's no good reason for us to continue processing it  
4. **Object to Processing** \- Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing  
5. **Request Restriction** \- Ask us to suspend processing in certain scenarios  
6. **Request Transfer** \- Receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format  
7. **Withdraw Consent** \- Withdraw consent at any time where we're relying on consent to process your data

### **Exercising Your Rights**

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **info@sixtyseconds.video**.

**No Fee Usually Required**

* You will not have to pay a fee to access your personal data or exercise other rights  
* We may charge a reasonable fee if your request is clearly unfounded, repetitive, or excessive

**Response Time**

* We try to respond to all legitimate requests within one month  
* If your request is particularly complex, it may take longer, and we will notify you

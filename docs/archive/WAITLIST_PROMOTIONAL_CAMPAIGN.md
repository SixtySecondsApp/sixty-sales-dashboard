## Appendix: Tone & Voice Guidelines

### Do

- Be honest and vulnerable (Andrew's real struggles)
- Focus on results (close rates, time saved)
- Use specific numbers and examples
- Acknowledge the pain before offering solutions
- Sound like a peer, not a vendor

### Don't

- Use buzzwords ("revolutionary," "game-changing")
- Over-promise or use hyperbolic claims
- Sound corporate or impersonal
- Lead with features instead of benefits
- Ignore the emotional toll of admin work
